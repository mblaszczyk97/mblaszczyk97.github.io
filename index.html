Hikari 2022

On August 19, 2022, another edition of the Hikari Festival was held. Cosplayers from all over Poland descended on the convention, and security and order were guarded by the organizers and volunteers led by the irreplaceable chief organizer Pyro. The price was 3 days - PLN 90 or in the VIP package PLN 300. In addition to many other benefits, the VIP package included an overnight option on the grounds of the Convention not available to ordinary buyers.
